MICHAEL
SCOFIELD

Michael J. Scofield is the main protagonist and principal character of the series. When his older brother, Lincoln Burrows, was sentenced to death for a crime he didn't commit, Michael created an elaborate plan to break him out. He obtained a jail sentence by robbing a bank to be incarcerated in Fox River State Penitentiary, the first step of his prison break plan.
